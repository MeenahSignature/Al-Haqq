about us

Al-Haqq Prayer Group of Canada is an Islamic prayer group who come together every Sunday to pray and discuss the importance of islam as a religion and our contributions as a group to our immediate community and beyond. We encourage the young generations to contribute to their immediate communities and aspire to add values in the community. We advice and give tips to becoming a better Muslim. We pray and help each other and those in need

mission and vision

Striving to be the world leading Islamic Organisation through which learned and spiritual generations evolved to develop excellent future generations tailored to Islamic paradigm and Beneficial to Mankind.
